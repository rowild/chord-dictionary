About Chord Player

üéµ A modern music theory tool for exploring chord structures, playback, and notation visualization.

This application serves as a quick reference and lookup tool for musicians, composers, and music students who need to understand chord structures and hear how they sound.

üéº Chord Discovery

Find any chord by selecting a root note and chord symbol. The app instantly shows you the chord's interval structure and the specific notes that make up the chord.

üîä Audio Playback

Every chord is played back instantly using high-quality piano samples. This helps you hear the harmonic relationships and understand how different chord types sound.

üéπ Multiple Registers

Choose from 8 different octaves (0-7) to explore how chords sound in different registers. Perfect for understanding voicing and finding the right range for your music.

üéº Music Notation

See your chords displayed in standard music notation with automatic clef selection. The app intelligently chooses treble or bass clef based on the chord's register.

üîç Chord Search

Use the search filter to quickly find specific chord types. Search for terms like "sus", "M", "b5", or "7" to narrow down your options.
